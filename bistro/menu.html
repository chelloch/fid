    Low and Slow

    posted by BGB, Nuvember 15, 2916

    This week I am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water beth that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In this book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.

    Next month, we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before November 30.
